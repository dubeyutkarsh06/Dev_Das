eCARE

Welcome to eCARE. A Complete guide to the pollution in the enviornment.

Our application seeks to provide the details about the air quality of one’s surrounding, it’s hazardous effects & sources. Apart from that it also gives us the concentration of various air pollutants in the surrounding. The application also provides the water quality index of the rivers in a given location. Using the water quality index we can get information about the quality of water and the various pollutants that are present in it.
