Welcome to eCARE!
A Complete Descriptiom of our app which aims to spread awareness about the pollution in the enviornment.

Our application seeks to provide the details about the air quality of one’s surrounding, it’s hazardous effects & sources. Apart from that it also gives us the concentration of various air pollutants in the surrounding. The application also provides the water quality index of the rivers in a given location. Using the water quality index we can get information about the quality of water and the various pollutants that are present in it.


Air Pollution & Air Quality Index

"Educating those currently at school about climate change will help to shape and sustain future policy-making". With this Aim our app makes people aware about the quality of air that is present in their locality. With the help of an API call, The app gets the location of the user and Air quality index of the surrounding is displayed. Using the Air quality index, user gets aware of the quality of air present in the surrounding and now he/she may take the necessary precautions.


Details about the Breezometer API used in the application can be found at the link given below:-

Water Pollution & Water Quality Index

Our app also aims to make people aware about the quality of water that is present in their locality. With the help of an API call, The app gets the location of the user and Water quality index of the surrounding water resources like Rivers, Lakes etc is displayed. Using the Water quality index, user gets aware of the quality of water present in their locality and now he/she can take the necessary precautions while using water resources.

Forum (Feed)

App also provides a forum (Feed) where people can post the pollution related problems in thier locality and actions against those problems will be taken accordingly. Apart from that we also have chatbot where people can give suggestions to the posted problems and if thier solutions are appropriate they will be rewarded accordingly. In this way climate change will help to shape and sustain future policy-making, and a broad public and international debate will support today’s policy-makers in taking strong action now.

Technology (Firebase)
